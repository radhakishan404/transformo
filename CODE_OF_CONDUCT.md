# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful and constructive in communication.
- Giving and accepting feedback gracefully.
- Focusing on what is best for the community and users.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, trolling, or insulting language.
- Personal attacks or discriminatory comments.
- Publishing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior and may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, and issues that do not align with this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

If you experience unacceptable behavior, report it through a private channel to the maintainers listed in the repository profile.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces and public interactions representing this project.
